facts about blue lock

bluelock is a manga series about football that breaks the mold of most shows about the sport.in contrast to the teamwork that other shows emphasize,bluelock teaches us to be egoistic but not selfish. this page will explore the masterpiece that is bluelock

bluelock is an amazing show that is not afraid to tackle difficult themes such as

  • self development
  • teamwork for one's own development
  • ego
etc and it has great art that surpasses the average comic that may encounter. it also has a wide cast of characters with different personalities and skill and all still stay revelant in the story despite the main characters getting the spot light
